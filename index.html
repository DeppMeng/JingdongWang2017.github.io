Biography

I am a Senior Researcher at Visual Computing Group, Microsoft Research Asia. My areas of interest include CNN architecture design, large-scale indexing, human understanding, and person re-identification. I have been serving/served as an Associate Editor of IEEE TMM, and an area chair (or SPC) of AAAI 2018, ICCV 2017, CVPR 2017, ECCV 2016 and ACM Multimedia 2015.
